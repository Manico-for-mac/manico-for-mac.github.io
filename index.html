How It Works

Step 1: Install and Launch

Drag Manico to Applications. It runs quietly in your menu bar.

Step 2: Customize Your Apps

Open settings. Assign apps to Option+1 through Option+0. Drag, drop, done.

Step 3: Switch Instantly

Press Option+Number. App launches or focuses in a flash. Hide icon for pristine menu bar.

Step 4: Fine-Tune (Optional)

Enable advanced options like hover switching in Settings. Your workflow, your rules.
